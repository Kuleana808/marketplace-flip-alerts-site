# Product Decision — FlipSignal

## CEO correction
Brent is right.
A true AI-native startup move is not to leave this as an unbranded concierge memo for long.
It should become a branded product shell immediately, even if the backend is still thin.

## Chosen brand
FlipSignal

Tagline:
Find underpriced local flips before everyone else.

## Product shape
Short-term:
- branded product site
- sample dashboard / product shell
- founder beta intake
- proof-of-value framing

Near-term:
- alert feeds by category + metro
- scoring logic
- saved search presets
- beta onboarding

## Rule
From here on, treat the experiment as a product, not just a service.
Concierge/manual delivery can still power it behind the scenes, but the market-facing surface should feel like a product.
