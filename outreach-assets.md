1. couch / furniture flippers

## Best initial positioning by segment

### Furniture / couch flippers
Subject / opener:
- Stop missing underpriced couches on Facebook Marketplace
- We send the local couch flips actually worth messaging first

Pain:
- deals disappear fast
- too much junk in saved searches
- need speed + clear spread

### Appliance flippers
Subject / opener:
- We flag the washer/dryer and appliance deals worth checking
- Less scrolling, more profitable appliance leads

Pain:
- time wasted checking low-quality listings
- hidden condition risk
- need quick spread logic

### Electronics / phone flippers
Subject / opener:
- Instant local sourcing edge for iPhones, MacBooks, consoles, and electronics
- We help you message the right listings first

Pain:
- extreme competition
- speed matters
- lots of false positives
